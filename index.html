Our Mission

Since the Tropila's move to America from Dublin Ireland in the 1960's, it has been their mission to share fresh quality,
authentic Irish cuisines passed down by multiple generations.
Using only grassfed meats, dairys, and organic grains and spices, our food exceeds expectations,
and will have you needing more of the the "sixth taste-bud"--
